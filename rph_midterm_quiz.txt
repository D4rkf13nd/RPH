RPH MIDTERM QUIZ

Instructions: Choose the best answer for each question.

1. According to Robert Heinlein, what happens to a generation that ignores history?
a) They become more progressive
b) They have no past and no future
c) They become more innovative
d) They develop better technology

2. Which of the following is NOT one of the three concepts of history?
a) Based on past events
b) Interpreted by someone, usually historians
c) Based on future predictions
d) Rely on data and documents called historical sources

3. In historical methodology, what are primary sources?
a) Scholarly works that analyze historical events
b) Documents created during modern times
c) Firsthand records created during the time being studied
d) Summarized compilations of historical data

4. What is a cartographer?
a) A person who studies maps
b) A person who draws or creates maps
c) A person who collects historical artifacts
d) A person who writes historical documents

5. What is the primary goal of historical criticism?
a) To discover modern interpretations of text
b) To discover the text's primitive or original historical context
c) To create new historical theories
d) To compare different historical accounts

6. Which of the following is NOT one of the 5 categories of primary sources?
a) Written documents
b) Numerical records
c) Secondary interpretations
d) Oral statements

7. Who was Antonio Pigafetta?
a) A Spanish conquistador
b) A Filipino historian
c) An Italian scholar and one of Magellan's chroniclers
d) A Portuguese explorer

8. According to Pigafetta's account, when was the first Mass in the Philippines celebrated?
a) March 31, 1521
b) April 1, 1521
c) March 30, 1521
d) April 15, 1521

9. What was "La Verdad"?
a) A Spanish newspaper
b) A newspaper published by Father Gomez before his execution
c) A government document
d) A revolutionary manifesto

10. What was the significance of the Cry of Balintawak/Pugadlawin?
a) The establishment of the Katipunan
b) The tearing of cedulas
c) The declaration of war against America
d) The signing of independence

11. Who painted "The Parisian Life"?
a) Jose Rizal
b) Juan Luna
c) Antonio Luna
d) Fernando Amorsolo

12. What were the three interpretations of "The Parisian Life"?
a) Historical, Religious, Political
b) Kwentong Barbero, Biographical and Tragic, Symbolic
c) Classical, Historical, Modern
d) Romantic, Tragic, Symbolic

13. Where was the Proclamation of Philippine Independence declared?
a) Manila
b) Kawit, Cavite
c) Malolos, Bulacan
d) Balintawak, Quezon City

14. During the Proclamation of Independence, what two important national symbols were first presented?
a) The Philippine flag and National ID
b) The Philippine flag and Lupang Hinirang
c) The National Seal and Lupang Hinirang
d) The National Anthem and National Seal

15. What was Corazon Aquino's memorable line in her speech to the U.S. Congress?
a) "Democracy is our strength"
b) "The Philippines shall rise again"
c) "I have not come here to beg for help. I have come to ask for justice"
d) "Freedom is our birthright"
